L(y^,y)=−(y logy^+(1−y) log(1−y^))\mathcal{L}(\hat y,y) = -(y\ log\hat y + (1-y)\ log(1 - \hat y)) L(y^​,y)=−(y logy^​+(1−y) log(1−y^​))
